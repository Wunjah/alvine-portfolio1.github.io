About Me

Hi there! My name is Wunjah Alvine and i'm a computer engineering student with a passion for front-end development and graphic designing. I've always been fascinated by how technology works and how it can be used to create beautiful and functional designs. My journey into the world of programming began when I was in high school. I started learning HTML and CSS and was amazed by how i could bring my ideas to life with just a few lines of code. Since then, I've been constantly learning and improving my skills in web development and graphic design. As a computer engineering student, I am learning about the fundamentals of computer science and software development. I am also taking courses in web development, which has allowed me gain a deeper understanding of how websites are built and how to optimize them for performance and user experience. Aside from my studies, I enjoy working on personal projects that allow me to explore my creativity and challenge myself. I love designing websites and creating unique user interfaces that are both visually appealing and intuitive to use. I also enjoy creating graphics and illustrations using tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. In my free time, I enjoy reading books on design and technology, watching tutorials on YouTube and attending tech conferences to learn from industry experts. I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a developer and designer. Thanks for taking the time to get to know me! I'm excited to see where my passion for technology and design takes me in the future.
